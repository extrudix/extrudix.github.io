Core Features

Everything in one place.
Nothing in the cloud.

Six deeply integrated modules covering every aspect of a 3D printing operation — from raw filament to finished invoice.

01

Print Job Log

Detailed records for every print — duration, material, nozzle config, slicing data, photos, and results. Multi-filament and multi-nozzle jobs supported.

02

Filament Inventory

Track every spool by brand, material, color, diameter, and weight remaining. Automatic low-stock visibility so you never get caught short.

03

Printer & Hardware

Full printer profiles with nozzle units, build plates, and hardware records. Built-in printer catalog covers all major brands.

04

Maintenance Scheduling

Set service intervals by hours, days, or print count. Get notified before problems arise and keep a full history of every task.

05

Cost & Analytics

True cost accounting with electricity matrices, labor rates, overhead, and 12-currency support. Export polished PDF reports.

06

Project Management

Group prints into projects with parts lists, cover photos, file attachments, and full cost rollups across every iteration.
